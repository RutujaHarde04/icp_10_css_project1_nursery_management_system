Tree Plantation Guidance


Tree plantation is crucial for improving the environment. Always select native species for better adaptation and growth. Prepare the soil well by adding compost or organic matter, and ensure proper spacing to allow trees to grow healthily.


Plant Caring Tips


Caring for plants involves consistent watering, regular pruning, and protection from pests. Ensure to follow the specific light and watering needs of your plants for optimal growth.
