General Plant Care Tips

To keep your plants healthy and thriving, consider the following tips:

  • Watering: Overwatering can harm roots, while underwatering can dehydrate the plant. Check soil moisture regularly.
  • Sunlight: Most plants need indirect sunlight. Observe specific requirements of each plant for optimal growth.
  • Fertilizing: Use the right type of fertilizer based on the plant type and growth stage.
  • Pruning: Trim dead leaves and branches to promote healthy growth.
  • Repotting: Repot plants when roots outgrow their containers to ensure they get sufficient space and nutrients.
